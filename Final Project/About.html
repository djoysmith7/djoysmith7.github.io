Sparkly Cupcakes is a nationally celebrated artisan bakery founded in Atlanta,Ga with an amazing staff of talented bakers. We are dedicated to small batch baking and to feeding our customer's with integrity. We maintain a philosophy of "real food". No shortening can be found in our kitchen.

We here at the Sparkly Cupcakes are honored to bake for you and we understand completely that without you, we wouldn't exist. We look forward to sharing space on your table for your life's celebrations and events!
